About This Platform

The QR+ Emergency Health ID platform is built to assist in emergencies. It generates QR codes that hold critical patient data like illness, allergies, contact, and real-time location. Once generated, these QR codes can be sent to the nearest hospital, enabling faster and better care for the patient on arrival.
